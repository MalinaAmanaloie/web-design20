Our Story

How it all started

  • The story of the à la maison cafe  started in 2017, when a team of Cluj investors created a unique concept which revolves around the passion for coffee. They never expected people to respond so well to their initiative, but the hard work and the services they offer seem to have paid off even better. This place is not just a coffee shop, this is the place where you can start your day in the nicest, coziest way possible, feeling like home.
  • "Maison" is the French word for "home". What better place to drink your coffee than in your own home? Even better, in a place that gives you the same feeling, but surrounded by friendly people, having everything you want specially prepared with love while enjoying the good music.
