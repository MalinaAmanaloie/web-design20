#coffeelover

One of our little projects, created on the initiative of one of our customers, meant for those who share the same passion for coffee as we do. All you have to do is bring your own mug, express your creative spirit by painting something that you love on it and then share a picture on social media using the hashtag #coffeelover. And we will make sure to serve your coffee everyday from your own personalized mug.
