Kettle Coffee

Another convenient and cheap method of making coffee at home is using the kettle.
Everyone has a kettle in their home, and if it is made of copper, you can make your coffee in an authentic Turkish style. Here is the recipe we propose for kettle coffee.

Utensils and ingredients

- Coffee: 7-10g/100g cold water, freshly roasted, freshly ground
- Grinding degree: maximum (Turkish style)
- Cold water
- Kettle
- Grinder
- Spoon for mixing
- Heat source

Step by step recipe

- Pour cold water about halfway up the kettle
- Put the coffee in the kettle and mix it
- Put the kettle on the heat source over low heat
- After 1 minute, some bubbles should form
- Don't let the coffee boil. If the bubbles are too aggressive, lift the kettle from the heat source - repeat this movement until after 2 minutes
- After the cream rises to the top of the kettle, take it off the heat source
- Quickly pour the coffee into two cups
- Wait a bit for the coffee grounds to settle and then you can enjoy your coffee.
